We're here to remind you of your rights!

  • If you are questioned or arrested by a police officer, you are not obligated to answer any questions.
  • If a police officer or immigration official comes to your home, they are not legally permitted to enter without a warrant
  • If an officer does have a warrant, make sure you get a receipt for any property that is confiscated
  • If you are arrested, you maintain the right to make a phone call
  • If a police officer tells you that you are not being arrested, you have the right to leave the situation
